About IC-BUSCLi 2026

A conference that unites CSI and ICIBMAH under one umbrella

One entry point

Visitors arrive here first, then choose the conference that fits—without hunting for links or switching pages.

Hybrid-ready

Designed for in-person attendees at PWCC and remote participants who join online.

Global collaboration

Organised by UniMAP (FPK) in collaboration with international partners.

Theme

Strategic leadership, sustainable innovation, and human-centred AI—framed for a disruptive global economy.

“Synergizing Strategic Leadership, Sustainable Innovation, and Human-Centred AI in a Disruptive Global Economy”

This theme blends business, communication, language/linguistics and technology—so the conversation stays practical, ethical, and human.

Tracks & clusters

10 tracks across two clusters—ICIBMAH (business/leadership/engineering) and CSI (communication/language/AI).
