General information

IC-BUSCLi 2026 serves as the umbrella gateway connecting two main conferences—CSI and ICIBMAH—under a shared theme and unified experience.

What is IC-BUSCLi?

A single entry point that brings together two conference streams. Guests choose their preferred track—without confusion, duplicated links, or extra pages.

Format & experience

Hybrid by design: attend physically at PWCC, Penang, or join online. Built to feel premium—clean visuals, subtle motion, and fast navigation.

Who should join?

Researchers, academics, practitioners, and postgraduate students across business, communication, language, technology, and human-centred AI.

Theme

Strategic leadership, sustainable innovation, and human-centred AI—framed for a disruptive global economy.

“Synergizing Strategic Leadership, Sustainable Innovation, and Human-Centred AI in a Disruptive Global Economy”

A theme that keeps the conversation practical and grounded—where innovation meets responsibility, and AI stays aligned with human values.

Tracks & clusters

10 tracks across two clusters—ICIBMAH (business/leadership/engineering) and CSI (communication/language/AI).
